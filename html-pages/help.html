Summary The Investment Browser will help us better understand how investment research affects research decisions. Thus, please use the Browser to do all of your investment research (or as much as possible). Our proprietary browser tracks your web activity. This tracking can be turned on and off at any time in the bottom right corner. Please keep tracking on as much as possible.

Step 1: Enter your username. If you have forgotten your username contact Jessen Hobson at JLhobson@illinois.edu. It is very important that you enter the correct username!

Step 2: After the Daily Picture, answer questions 1 and 2 carefully and completely. Please don't skip this step--it is critically important for us to know what research you are doing.

Step 3: Please update your responses to these questions each time you change what you are researching. To do this, click on "Research Purpose" and "Company" at the bottom of the browser.

Please direct any questions or comments to JLhobson@illinois.edu. Thank you for your participation.
